# Questionnaire Built With Science - Extraction des questions

Ce document recense l'intégralité des questions posées lors du tunnel de quiz de [Built With Science](https://quiz.builtwithscience.com/page/1), ainsi que les descriptions/sous-textes associés.

1. **What is your main fitness goal right now?**
   *Sous-texte :* This helps tailor our science-backed recommendations to your goal.
2. **What gender do you identify with?**
   *Sous-texte :* Some aspects of your plan adapt based on gender-specific physiology.
3. **What is your age range?**
   *Sous-texte :* This helps us factor in age-related changes in your metabolism.
4. **What is your height?**
   *Sous-texte :* This helps us spot trends across thousands of users on similar journeys.
5. **What is your current weight?**
   *Sous-texte :* We don’t mean to pry – this just helps us build a plan that’s right for you.
6. **What should we call you?** 
   *(Étape de personnalisation : prénom)*
7. **What best matches your current body type?**
   *Sous-texte :* No right or wrong – we use this to better understand your starting point.
8. **Which image best reflects your current body fat level?**
   *Sous-texte :* It doesn’t need to be exact, just choose the one that feels closest.
9. **What is the ideal body fat % you’d like to reach?**
   *Sous-texte :* We’ll need this to personalize your plan.
10. **How would you describe your training and nutrition?**
    *Sous-texte :* This helps us understand your current habits, and how best to help.
11. **What is affecting your consistency most?**
    *Sous-texte :* Helps us understand how we can help.
12. **What area(s) are the most difficult to lose fat from?**
    *Sous-texte :* Select up to 3. This helps us see where most people face challenges.
13. **What muscle group do you want to prioritize?**
    *Sous-texte :* This helps us create a program that targets your specific goals.
14. **What’s your gym experience?**
    *Sous-texte :* Be honest—your plan adapts to your level.
15. **Your goal timeline is ready.**
    *Sous-texte :* Enter your email to see how long it will take to reach your goal. *(Étape de capture d'email)*
